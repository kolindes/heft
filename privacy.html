Heft — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-25_

Heft is designed to be private by default. Most users' data never leaves their device.

What the app does with your data

Meal photos.

  • On-device mode (default): Your photo is analyzed entirely on your iPhone using Apple's Vision framework and a bundled nutrition database. The photo and its analysis never leave your device.
  • Optional cloud mode: If you choose to configure a third-party AI provider in Settings (e.g. OpenAI or another OpenAI-compatible service) and supply your own API key, your meal photo is sent to that provider you selected for analysis — and only after you explicitly tap to allow it the first time. We name the provider before any photo is sent. We do not control and are not responsible for that provider's data practices; please review their privacy policy. Heft itself does not store your photos on any server.

Apple Health.

  • With your permission, Heft writes the nutrition of meals you log (calories, macronutrients, vitamins, and minerals) to Apple Health, and reads your daily dietary-energy total to show today's progress.
  • This data is stored by Apple Health on your device. Heft never uses Health data for advertising or marketing, and never shares or sells it.

Meal history.

  • Meals you log are stored only on your device (including the photo and nutrition breakdown) so you can review, edit, or delete them. Deleting a meal also removes its entry from Apple Health.

API keys.

  • If you add a provider API key, it is stored in the device Keychain and is never transmitted anywhere except, as a bearer credential, to the provider you configured.

What we do NOT do

  • No user accounts. No sign-in.
  • No analytics, no advertising, no third-party tracking SDKs.
  • No collection of your data on our servers (we operate none).
  • We do not sell or share your personal data.

Children

Heft is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date will change accordingly.
